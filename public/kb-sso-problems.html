SSO Problems

Login loops, stale sessions, app not showing in IdP.

Clear session / cookies

Resolve stale SSO state.

  1. Sign out of all SSO tabs
  2. Clear IdP cookies and site data
  3. Retry login in incognito or different browser

Check app assignment

User/app mapping in IdP.

  1. Confirm user is in correct group/role
  2. Verify app is assigned and active
  3. Sync groups; wait or force refresh

Entitlements

Missing app/role after SSO.

  1. Check entitlement group membership
  2. Reapply role claim mappings
  3. Have user relog to refresh tokens

Common errors

“App not assigned”, “Too many redirects”.

  • Reset device clock/time sync
  • Disable conflicting VPN/proxy
  • Try fallback auth (local account) if allowed
